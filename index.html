What is Agentix.bio?

Agentix.bio is a platform of modular AI agents designed to support researchers, scientists, and innovators in the life sciences. Whether you're building workflows, managing data, writing methods sections, or tracking insights, Agentix agents adapt to your environment — always keeping a human in the loop.

Featured Agent: Ava

Ava helps you design and document reproducible analysis pipelines in CWL, WDL, or Nextflow. She understands your research goals and suggests tools, optimizes execution plans, and generates code and documentation.
